## 2. The Service — what Tarf is

Tarf is a free wellness app whose core is an **activity-aware 20-20-20 eye-break** fused with a calm
**dhikr "repeat-after-me"** screen, surrounded by optional productivity tools (Focus/Pomodoro, Timer, Alarm,
Stopwatch, Insights, To-dos), with offline-first local storage and optional cloud sync for signed-in users.

- **Guest mode:** the eye-care + dhikr loop runs fully **offline**, no account required.
- **Signed-in mode:** unlocks the productivity tools and cross-device sync.

**Tarf is a wellness and reminder aid, not a medical device.** It does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any
eye condition or other illness. See §10.
